Our Masala Fries
Our masala fries are a delightful appetizer that combines the crispy texture of perfectly fried potato strips with a tantalizing blend of aromatic spices. Each bite is a burst of flavor, as cumin, coriander, red chili powder, and turmeric create a harmonious symphony of taste. The addition of chaat masala adds a tangy and slightly tangy kick, elevating the overall experience. Whether enjoyed on their own or paired with a refreshing lemon wedge, our masala fries are a crowd-pleasing snack that will leave you craving more.
